About

Built for students. Designed for everyone.

This website was made originally for students who have a hard time managing their fitness goals and planning their meals accordingly, in addition to getting the sufficient amount of sleep needed to grow, function throughout the day, and recover from fatigue caused by their workouts. However, this is a tool made for anyone with any type of schedule or lifestyle.

This website includes:

  • Template workouts
  • Exercise logging
  • Meal planning
  • Progress tracking

In conclusion, this fitness planner demonstrates how combining workout templates, meal tracking, and progress monitoring into a single intuitive platform can significantly improve user consistency and health awareness.
